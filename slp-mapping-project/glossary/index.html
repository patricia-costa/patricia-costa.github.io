PORTUGUESE BURGHERS
Eurasian community of Portuguese descent that maintains a set of idiosyncratic cultural traditions, chief among them the use of Sri Lanka Portuguese. Predominantly Catholic in religion.
SRI LANKA PORTUGUESE

An endangered and minority language spoken in Sri Lanka by the Portuguese Burghers. ISO 639-3: idb; Glottolog mala1544.

SPEAKERS

The category “speakers” includes survey respondents who declared ‘I understand and speak Sri Lanka Portuguese fluently’ or ‘I understand and speak with little difficulty’.

SEMI-SPEAKERS

The category “semi-speakers” includes survey respondents who declared ‘I understand and speak with some difficulty”.

NON-SPEAKERS

The category “non-speakers” includes survey respondents who declared ‘I can’t understand or speak”.

NA

Not available or no answer, i.e. respondents who did not answer or did not to so properly.

GRAMA NILADHARI

A sub-unit of divisional secretariats. The Sri Lankan administrative system is divided into provinces (9), districts (25), divisional secretariats (331) and grama niladharis (14,022).
